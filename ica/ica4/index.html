  • Identity and Culture

    My color choice reflects who I am based on the fact that orange is my favorite color. I based my color scheme off of that, blue is complimentary and red is right next to orange. I also like the color scheme that includes more grayscale colors, so I included something close to black and white (this also helps for contrast). The Lexend Deca font feels most authentic to me. I like its simplicity and readability.

  • Accessibility Considerations

    The accessibility requires changed how I created the design palette especially when it came to choosing colors. I had to make sure I had two colors that could contrast easily with almost all of the other colors. The color palette was the most challenging aspect of this design choice in relation to accessbility at the contrast needed to be high enough.

  • Design Process

    I have already done some designing similar to this before so I was not too surprised. However, adding a font to the webpage was much simpler than I was expecting. Being able to import the font in such a simple way, just copying the code from google fonts, made the styling really efficient. I was also surprised to hear that 3 colors was probably too little for a website. I am used to 3 colors, and 5 is almost overwhelming when trying to build something cohesive.

  • Future Applications

    I could refine this system for future contexts by simply using this html document as a basis for creating a palette. It is nice and readable, it deals with multiple fonts and even styling for something like a button. The aspect of the system that feels the most flexible feels to me like the color palette. Although a change to the color palette would have to apply to mutliple different feilds, it is something that does not feel overwhelming, although it could take time. The least flexible aspect of this system to me is likely the margins that is the spacing between different fields. Messing with the margins will have a different impact on different systems, so changing the margins might affect that portability.
